BIO

As a highly experienced Lecturer in Management, I have had the privilege of teaching over 1000 students to date in several universities including, Manchester Metropolitan University, Arden University and BPP University. I have taught across various levels of education, from Foundation Degree to Master's Degree levels in the UK. Additionally, I have marked assessments and supervised dissertations for Master's students, allowing me to gain a deep understanding of what it takes to achieve academic success in this field. My primary subjects have been, HRM and Organization Behaviour. Starting in October 2023, I will be embarking on a new chapter as a PhD Researcher and Graduate Teaching Fellow at the University of Liverpool School of Management, where my research will focus on Equality, Diversity, Inclusion, and Justice. Specifically, I will be exploring the intersectionality of gender, race, and sexual orientation and how they contribute to microaggressions in the workplace. Through my research, I hope to promote social justice and create more inclusive environments for all individuals. This is a fully funded position. I will also be involved in teaching at the university.
